"Days of the week" task

Practice trial

For the first task, you will see a day of the week written in the center of the screen. You will have to tell whether this day is in next (future) or previous (past) two days.

You will now start with a practice trial. Your answers now won't be recorded. Its only purpose is to make sure you know how the task work.

There are buttons at the bottom of the screen. They are rather a reminder of which is left or right. Please respond with your keyboard: with the left or right arrow key. Place your left index finger on the left arrow key and your right index finger on the right arrow key.


Please press the right arrow key to start the practice trial.
